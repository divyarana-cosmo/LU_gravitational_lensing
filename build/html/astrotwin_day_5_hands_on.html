Day 5 - Environmental dependence of matter profiles

In this session, we will study the weak lensing signal around the satellite galaxies in a cluster environment. We will first make some scatter plots and decide on the selection criteria for our satellite galaxies. As before, we will then use them as lenses and measure the weak-lensing signal around them.

The catalog data is available by the name “redmapper_satellites.dat”

Lets first make a scatter plot

Here we will make the plot between redshift and projected separation of satellite from the cluster center. We will then select the sample using the cuts on the separation.
